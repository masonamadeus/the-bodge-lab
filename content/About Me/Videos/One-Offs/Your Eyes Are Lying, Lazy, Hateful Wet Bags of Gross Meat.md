1. There are actually five types of melanin: eumelanin, pheomelanin, neuromelanin, allomelanin and pyomelanin. 2. The effect that causes your irises to appear blue/green/etc is called Tyndall Scattering. The effect that causes the sky to appear blue is called Rayleigh Scattering. The mechanism by which the effect of 'color-shifting' takes place is different between the two, but the effect itself is roughly the same. Also the sun is not actually impossible. It is, in fact, QUITE possible. Some would say indisputably possible.
