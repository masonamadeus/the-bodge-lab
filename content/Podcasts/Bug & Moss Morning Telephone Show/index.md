# The Bug & Moss Morning Telephone Show

**It's like an old school call-in radio show!**

Join Bug (Brooke Jennett) and Moss (Mason Amadeus) **every weekday morning** for a silly, interactive, cozy ramble of a podcast.

It's a nice **30-minute show** to put on in the background of your morning routine!

**Leave a voicemail or send a text to +1 (848) MOSS-BUG**
