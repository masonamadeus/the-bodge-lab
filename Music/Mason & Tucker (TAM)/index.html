Mason & Tucker

This is an album of music that my best friend Tucker and I wrote, recorded, and produced ourselves. This was almost entirely made before I really knew anything about audio, and if I had a time machine (and the ) I would go back and remix it all.

I lost ALL of our original files for this when I wiped my old hard drive. I really thought I had backed it all up too. This was one of those moments that really drove home the importance of keeping copies of important digital artifacts.

If you want to check out other stuff we created together, you can peek at our bandcamp page: https://masonandtucker.bandcamp.com!
